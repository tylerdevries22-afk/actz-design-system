What Georgetown captures today

12 million cars a year — and a sliver of the spend.

Maya is one of roughly 12 million vehicles a year. Fewer than 1 in 50 actually stops — and of the bookings that do happen, 15–30% leaks straight back out to the OTAs. Georgetown carries the traffic, the parking, and the trail wear, but keeps only a sliver of the spend.

Sources: CDOT I-70 corridor traffic counts · Colorado Tourism Office / Dean Runyan travel-impact data · Georgetown Loop Railroad & Gateway Visitor Center · published OTA commission rates. OTA-leakage dollar figure is a conservative estimate.

15–30%of every OTA booking leaves town as commission — an est. $1M+ a year out of Georgetown
