Arrow spins · Waves radiate
The compass arrow rotates at 3.6s/rev (locked to the 0.6s wave interval × 6). Ten waves are born one-at-a-time from the triangle centre, cycling white → red → gold → blue. Each colored ring answers with a soft pulse as a wave crosses it.

Ensemble deceleration
On ACTZSplash.appReady(): the arrow captures its current rotation, then animates 1–2 full extra turns before easing onto true north (0°). Waves fade out over 1.4s. The three rings stop pulsing simultaneously — a single shared decel.
